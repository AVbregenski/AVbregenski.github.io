Hello!

Nice to meet you! I’m Amanda Bregenski, a 19-year-old Brazilian currently living in the Netherlands. Since 2022, I've been calling this beautiful country my home. I've been working in the horeca sector at a restaurant in Goes, where I've gained valuable experience and enjoyed interacting with people from various backgrounds. One of my favorite things to do here is to explore the local culture and cuisine. I love going out with friends and discovering new places, whether it’s a cozy café or a picturesque town. Traveling around the Netherlands has been a fantastic experience; each city and village has its own unique charm.

I’m also passionate about learning Dutch. The language is both fascinating and challenging, but I'm making great progress. Immersing myself in the language has made my experience here even more rewarding, and I enjoy practicing Dutch in my daily interactions.

Living in the Netherlands has broadened my horizons and given me a new perspective on life. I’m excited to continue exploring, learning, and making the most of my time here.

Movie:
