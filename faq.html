Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ? To print using Mobility Print, follow these steps:
  • Visit the hz mynetpay.n website and select "Mobility Print."
  • Choose your current software and install the Mobility Print app.
  • Select the file(s) you want to print and choose the "HZ Printer."
  • Find a printer and either scan your student card or enter your HZ login credentials.
  • Click on "Print Release," select your file, adjust your settings as needed, and press "Print."
    •
    How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ? To scan documents using a printer, follow these steps:
  • Log in to the printer using your HZ student card or your username and password.
  • Choose the "Scan" option.
  • Place the documents you want to scan on the feeder or glass plate.
  • Adjust your preferred settings and press "Start."
  • Remember to log out when you're finished.
    • How can I buy something on the HZ web shop? To make a purchase on the HZ webshop, follow these steps:
  • Visit webshop.hz.nl and use the navigation bar to choose between "HZ," "HZ Cult," and "HZ Sport."
  • Scroll down to browse the available products and select the items you wish to buy.
  • After making your selections, go to your shopping cart and click on "Checkout."
  • Complete the payment process to finalize your purchase.
    •
    How can you book a project space? To reserve a meeting or project room through MyHiZ, follow these steps:
  • Log in to MyHiZ and go to the personal menu, then select the self-service portal.
  • Choose "Reservations," then "Reserve a meeting or project room," and finally "Reserve a room."
  • On the top left, select the desired date, and the top row will display available times.
  • Click and drag to select a time slot in your preferred room.
  • Click "Next," fill in your information, and confirm the reservation.
    •
    What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot? Parking is not available at the Groene Woud campus. Instead, you can use the parking lot on Kousteensedik, which offers spaces for visitors, staff, and students of Het Groene Woud. This parking area is an 8-minute walk from the HZ campus. To obtain an exit card, show your entry card at the JRCZ service desk, located next to the HZ building.
